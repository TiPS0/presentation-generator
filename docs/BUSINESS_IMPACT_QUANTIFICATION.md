# 2. Revenue Growth Model

## 2.1 Increased Daily Store Visits

### Current Situation (Example Assumption)

- 10–15 stores per day
- Average = 12 stores/day

### With Smart Route Optimization

If routing efficiency saves:

- 45–60 minutes per day
  (from traffic avoidance + optimized stop sequence)

Sales capacity may increase by:

- +2 to +3 additional store visits/day

### New Estimated Capacity

- 14–15+ stores/day
- ~15–25% increase in visit capacity

---

## 2.2 Revenue Expansion Example

Assumption:

- Average revenue per store visit = $300
- 2 additional stores/day
- 22 working days/month

Calculation:

2 stores × $300 × 22 days  
= $13,200 additional revenue per month

If 3 additional stores/day:

3 × $300 × 22  
= $19,800 additional revenue per month

---

## 2.3 Upsell & Promotion Impact

With:

- Structured promotion display
- Real-time product visibility
- Alert messaging engagement

If average order size increases by 5–10%:

Example:
$300 → increases to $315–$330

If 12 stores/day × 22 days = 264 stores/month

5% increase:
$15 × 264 = $3,960 additional revenue/month

10% increase:
$30 × 264 = $7,920 additional revenue/month

# 3. New Customer Acquisition Impact

# 4. Cost Optimization Model

## 4.1 EV Travel Efficiency

Benefits:

- Reduced unnecessary detours
- Reduced idle driving
- Optimized energy usage

If route efficiency reduces:

- 10% driving distance

This leads to:

- Lower electricity consumption
- Reduced vehicle wear
- Lower maintenance frequency

Even small % reductions accumulate annually.

---

## 4.2 Waiting Time Reduction

Current hidden cost:

- Customer not ready
- Communication delays
- Manual coordination

With Alert Messaging:

- “Van arriving in 10 minutes”
- Store prepares in advance

If:
10 stores × 7 minutes saved  
= 70 minutes/day saved

Equivalent to:
+1 additional selling hour per day

This directly supports increased store visits.

---

## 4.3 Administrative Efficiency

Reduced:

- Manual route adjustment
- Repeated messaging
- Unstructured communication

Impact:

- More time focused on selling
- Less operational friction
- Cleaner workflow between field and HQ

## â“ FAQ

### Q: Why can't I reply to the message?

**A:** Because another Agent replied first, assigning the room to them uniquely. You must wait for them to Transfer it to you.

### Q: Why is the Close button Disabled?

**A:** You must reply to every customer message before you can close the Ticket.

### Q: What should I do after receiving a Transfer?

**A:**

1. Click Notification or go to chat room from "Mine" tab.
2. Read the Note from the Transferring Agent.
3. Continue responding to the customer.

### Q: Customer sends a new message after I own the room?

**A:** The new Ticket will be automatically assigned to you. No extra action needed.

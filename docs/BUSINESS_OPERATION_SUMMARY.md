# 1. Company Overview

**Company Name:** Patanayont  
**Project Name:** Partvans

**Business Model Context:**

- Automotive spare parts distribution
- Mobile sales model using EV vans
- Sales representatives operate as both:
  - Order fulfillment sales
  - Active product promoters
  - New customer developers

---

# 2. Core Operational Structure

## 2.1 Daily Sales Assignment Process (Morning at HQ)

Each morning, the Sales Representative:

- Arrives at HQ
- Receives:
  - Tablet device
  - Physical tax invoices (for previous transactions)
  - Assigned sales tasks
- Prepares for field operation using an EV van

Sales role is NOT a delivery rider.
The role includes:

- Selling requested products
- Proactively promoting additional products
- Expanding customer base

---

# 3. Inventory & Logistics Preparation

## 3.1 Product Loading Process

- Logistics team prepares goods for each van.
- Product list originates from:
  - Lead / Project Supervisor
  - Based on requested SKUs
  - Defined by Min / Max stock levels for multiple items

## 3.2 Vehicle Constraints

Vehicle type: EV (Electric Van)

Operational considerations:

- Travel distance calculation
- Vehicle weight (including inventory)
- Battery capacity management
- Route efficiency
- Load planning impact on energy consumption

---

# 4. Route Planning & Assignment

## 4.1 Route Management

- Routes are primarily created by the Lead.
- Routes are planned:
  - Monthly
  - Or adjusted based on operational status

## 4.2 On-Road Adjustments

If unexpected events occur:

- Traffic congestion
- Road construction
- Delays
- Route disruptions

Sales representative must:

- Manually adjust route
- Independently manage situational decisions

---

# 5. Field Sales Execution

## 5.1 At Customer Location

Sales responsibilities:

- Deliver requested spare parts
- Promote additional products
- Present promotions and upsell opportunities
- Expand order size where possible

## 5.2 Transaction Handling

When a sale is completed:

- Collect payment
- Take photo of payment slip
- Prepare documentation for future tax invoice issuance
- Deliver previous tax invoices to returning customers

Sales process continues across multiple stores throughout the day.

---

# 6. End-of-Day HQ Process

After returning to HQ:

## 6.1 Inventory Reconciliation

- Check remaining stock inside EV van
- Compare against expected stock levels

## 6.2 Sales Feedback & Reporting

- Sales provides feedback to Lead
- Lead collects:
  - Sales data
  - Product performance insights
  - Route performance information

## 6.3 Data Analysis

Lead performs:

- Sales analytics
- Demand evaluation
- Inventory planning
- Preparation for future sales cycles

---

# 7. Billing & Financial Workflow

After end-of-day reconciliation:

- Sales submits photographed payment slips
- Billing team:
  - Generates official tax invoices
- Accounting / Finance team:
  - Processes financial records
  - Completes accounting documentation
